MORNING MEDICATIONS

6:00 AM

  • Omeprazole
  • Insulin
  • Zrytec

Take 1 Omeprazole and check your blood sugar 30 mins. BEFORE eating.

Take Zrytec if you're having allergies

AFTERNOON MEDICATIONS

12:00 PM

  • Tylenol
  • Nebulizer
  • Enalapril

Take 2 Tylenol if you're having any mild pain.

Check your blood pressure-Take 1 Enalapril.

Do your Nebulizer if you're short of breath.

EVENING MEDICATIONS

8:00 PM

  • Insulin
  • Enalapril
  • Melatonin

Check your blood sugar
Check your blood pressure-Take 1 Enalapril.

Take 1 Melatonin if you aren't sleepy.

**Enalapril can affect your heart or your electrolyte levels. Call your doctor if you have chest pain**
